# 🎓 College Game Plan (June 2026 → Graduation ~late 2027) — v1.1

Your mission for the next ~18 months: **build the habit, not the balance.** The peso amount while
you're a student is small — what you're really doing is installing a system that will run for 20+
years once your income kicks in. Consistency now > optimization.

Strategy: **Plan 2.1 — Max Growth, Trusted Optimizer.** 88% global equity (**VT, the all-world
Vanguard ETF, via GoTrade**) / 12% PH REITs (60% AREIT / 40% CREIT, via DragonFi).
Contribution: **₱5,000/week** (half your ₱10k allowance). 100% invested, strict monthly buys.

> **Why v1.1 (2026-06-11):** the BPI feeder fund's fee was confirmed at **1.5%/yr** — roughly a
> ₱3M drag on the age-45 goal if kept for 21 years. GoTrade was due-diligenced: your account is
> opened in **your own name at Alpaca Securities LLC** (US FINRA/SIPC member, $500k protection,
> segregated assets), so GoTrade going bust would be a hassle, not a loss. VT replaces the S&P 500
> feeder as the core: all-world, ~9,000 companies, 0.06–0.07% fee — the same shape Norway's
> $2T sovereign fund and Japan's GPIF hold.

## PHASE 0 — Setup (do once, in the next 1–2 weeks)
1. **Open a DragonFi account** (₱1,000 to start) — for the REIT sleeve.
2. **Open a GoTrade account** (download app, KYC, link funding) — for the global sleeve.
   You'll sign the Alpaca Customer Agreement; that's the US broker that actually holds your shares.
3. **Pick your contribution day** — the day your allowance lands. Same day every week. Treat the
   ₱5,000 like a bill you pay yourself first, before any spending.
4. **(Optional) Keep a tiny buffer** — hold ~₱2,000–3,000 in cash so a surprise expense never forces
   you to sell investments.
5. **Fallback rule:** if GoTrade isn't verified and funded within one month, put that month's global
   money into the BPI US Equity Index Feeder Fund on DragonFi instead (it's a parking spot —
   redemptions are untaxed, so switching later is cheap). Never let setup stall the habit.

## PHASE 1 — The monthly rhythm (every month, for all 18 months)
Buying weekly in tiny amounts wastes fees, so **batch it monthly**:
- Park your ₱5,000/week as it arrives. Once a month (~₱20,000 accumulated), invest:
  - **~₱17,600 → VT on GoTrade** (the 88% growth engine; fractional, so the exact peso amount
    always fits). Deposit costs ₱45 + a small FX spread — that's the price of the pipe.
  - **~₱2,400 → REITs on DragonFi**, 60% AREIT / 40% CREIT. Board lots run ≈ ₱3,200–3,800, so let
    the REIT cash build and **alternate** — one month buy a CREIT board lot, the next an AREIT board
    lot. Over time that tracks the 60/40 target without wasting commissions on tiny buys.
- **Reinvest every dividend.** AREIT and CREIT pay quarterly cash (minus 10% tax) — roll it straight
  back in. VT pays quarterly USD dividends (30% withheld at source) — reinvest them inside GoTrade.

## PHASE 2 — The discipline (the part that actually matters)
- ✅ **Automate / pre-commit.** Move the ₱5,000 the moment the allowance arrives.
- ✅ **Don't watch prices daily.** A monthly glance is enough. Markets will dip — that's normal and
  is *cheaper shares for you.*
- ✅ **Never sell the core.** No "I'll time it" selling. The plan is buy-and-hold for 10–20 years.
- ✅ **Raise the contribution whenever you can** — side gigs, bigger allowance, gifts. Every extra
  peso invested at 24 is worth the most.
- ❌ **No chasing.** Ignore hot stocks, crypto tips, "guaranteed" returns, and Signature Portfolio
  streaks. Stick to the two sleeves.

## PHASE 3 — Check-ins (with me, in this folder)
- **Monthly:** after you invest, tell me what you bought — I update `holdings.json` + the dashboard.
- **Every ~2 weeks or monthly:** full research/risk/forecast cycle (now includes **Institutional

- **Quarterly:** quick rebalance check — if a sleeve drifts >5 percentage points off target, nudge new
  contributions toward the underweight one (don't sell to rebalance — just steer fresh money). This
  applies both global-vs-REIT and AREIT-vs-CREIT within the sleeve.

## PHASE 4 — Graduation (~late 2027): level up
When you start earning:
1. **Raise contributions hard** — this is when the plan gets serious. Aim well above ₱5k/week.
2. **Build a real emergency fund** from salary (3–6 months expenses) — separate from investments.
3. **Open IBKR** and migrate the global core from VT (US-domiciled) into **VWRA (Irish-domiciled
   all-world, accumulating)** — 15% dividend withholding instead of 30%, and **zero US estate-tax
   exposure. Hard rule: migrate before the GoTrade balance approaches $60,000 (~₱3.4M).**
4. **Re-run the whole strategy** with me using your real income and updated goals.
