🎓 College Game Plan — June 2026 → Graduation (~late 2027)

The mindset: for the next ~18 months you're building the habit, not the balance. The peso amounts are small now — the point is to install a system that runs for 20+ years once you're earning. Consistency beats optimization.

Strategy: Plan 2 — Max Growth · 90% global equity / 10% PH REITs · ₱5,000/week.

Phase 0 — Setup (next 1–2 weeks, once)

  • Open a DragonFi account (₱1,000 to start) — one platform, both sleeves.
  • Check the feeder fund fee in-app (sources ranged 0.55%–1.5%) and report it back.
  • Pick your contribution day = the day your allowance lands. Pay yourself the ₱5,000 first.
  • (Optional) Keep a tiny ₱2–3k cash buffer so a surprise expense never forces a sale.

Phase 1 — Monthly rhythm

Buying weekly wastes commissions, so batch monthly. Each month (~₱20,000):

  • ~₱18,000 → BPI US Equity Index Feeder Fund (the 90% growth engine)
  • ~₱2,000 → AREIT — buy when you've saved a board lot (100 sh ≈ ₱3,800), so ~every 1–2 months
  • Reinvest every dividend the moment it lands — that's the compounding flywheel

Phase 2 — The discipline (what actually decides the outcome)

  • ✅ Move the ₱5,000 the instant the allowance arrives
  • ✅ Don't watch prices daily — dips just mean cheaper shares for you
  • ✅ Never sell the core — no timing, no panic; hold 10–20 years
  • ✅ Raise contributions whenever you can — a peso invested at 24 is your most valuable peso
  • ❌ No chasing hot stocks / crypto / "guaranteed" tips — just the two sleeves

Phase 3 — Check-ins (with Claude, in this folder)

  • Monthly: report what you bought → holdings + dashboard get updated
  • Say "run my update" every ~2 weeks for a full research/risk/forecast cycle
  • When the REIT sleeve passes ~₱20–30k → add CREIT (60/40 AREIT/CREIT)
  • Quarterly: rebalance check — steer fresh money to the underweight sleeve (don't sell)

Phase 4 — Graduation (~late 2027): level up 🚀

  • Raise contributions hard — this is when the plan gets serious
  • Build a real emergency fund (3–6 months) from salary, separate from investments
  • Open IBKR → move the global core into an Irish-domiciled ETF (VWRA/CSPX): 15% withholding, no US estate tax, lower fee
  • Re-run the whole strategy with your real income and updated goals
